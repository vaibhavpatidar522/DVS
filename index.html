Features

Everything You Need in a Modern and Digital Voting System

Secure Voting

Traditional voting systems often face security vulnerabilities, risking the integrity of election results. BEIS employs blockchain technology to fortify the security of electoral processes, ensuring that votes are securely recorded and tamper-proof.

Integrity

Through the immutable nature of blockchain, each vote cast through BEIS is recorded transparently and cannot be altered or tampered with. This ensures that the electoral process remains free from manipulation, bolstering public trust and confidence in the democratic system.

Accessibility

Through its user-friendly web interface, voters can securely cast their ballots from any location with internet access. By removing geographical and logistical barriers, BEIS promotes inclusivity and ensures that every eligible voter can exercise their right to vote effectively.

How to Vote

Connect MetaMask to Vote

To participate in the voting process, please connect your MetaMask wallet. This ensures secure and verified transactions, allowing you to cast your vote seamlessly. Follow the steps below to connect your MetaMask wallet and exercise your voting rights.

Enter Aadhaar to Retrieve City & State for Voting

Enter your Aadhaar details to retrieve your city and state information and proceed with the voting process

List Candidates to Vote with MetaMask

Once you enter the Aadhar Details, click on List Candidate,then you can securely cast your vote using MetaMask. This ensures a seamless and verified voting process.
